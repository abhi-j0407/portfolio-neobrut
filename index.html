About Me

Hello! I am Abhishek, a 26-year-old web developer specializing in front-end development. Hailing from the vibrant streets of Agra, I possess an innate appreciation for captivating aesthetics and the art of crafting exceptional creations.

I embarked on my journey as a Frontend Web Developer at Senpiper Technologies, a dynamic startup headquartered in Gurugram. My responsibilities there included participation in the development of their proprietary low code platform and spearheading the development of the company's website from its inception. Prior to this, I honed my skills as a Programmer Analyst at Cognizant, where I actively contributed to compliance resolutions, executed database updates for a prestigious publishing firm.

When I'm not immersed in the latest happenings of the web world, you'll find me at the gym, determined to shed those extra kilograms acquired during long hours at the desk. Aslo a connoisseur of delightfully terrible Valorant matches, oddly satisfying memes, and peculiar YouTube videos.
